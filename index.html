About Us

Steddy Trading Company Limited is a registered surveying and estate agency firm offering professional land and property solutions in East Africa. With over 30 years of experience, we provide a wide range of surveying and estate agency services, committed to accuracy, efficiency, and excellence.

Our Services

  • Cadastral Surveys

    For urban and rural titles, ensuring accurate and legally compliant land boundaries.

  • Topographical Surveys

    Detailed mapping of land contours, elevations, and natural features for development planning.

  • Engineering Surveys

    Precise surveys for major projects including dams, roads, and earth dams.

  • Service Line Surveys

    Accurate mapping for power lines, sewer systems, and other utility suppliers.

  • Agricultural Surveys

    Surveys for potential agriculture, including soil sampling and fertilizer prescriptions.

  • Demarcation & Setting Out

    For access roads and property boundaries, ensuring clear and precise demarcation.

  • Property Sales & Letting

    Assisting clients in buying, selling, and renting residential and commercial properties.

  • Property Management

    Comprehensive management of properties, including tenant screening and maintenance.

  • Valuation Services

    Professional valuation of properties for various purposes, including mortgages and sales.

  • Feasibility Studies

    Providing expert analysis to determine the viability of property and land projects.

  • Project Supervision

    Overseeing construction and development projects to ensure they meet standards.

  • Land Use Planning

    Consultancy on optimal land use strategies for various purposes.
